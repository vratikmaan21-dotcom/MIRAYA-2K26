About the Night

Welcome to the Family

MIRAYA 2K26 is more than a party — it's a tradition. Every year, the seniors of CVAS Bikaner roll out the red carpet for our newest members, the Batch of 2026, with an evening of warmth, culture, and celebration.

Expect dazzling performances, roaring laughter, heartfelt felicitations, and a night that will stay in your heart long after the music fades.

"From strangers to a family — the journey begins tonight."
